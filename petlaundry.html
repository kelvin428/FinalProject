Pet Laundry

  • Pet Laundry Without The Headache

    Stop cleaning pet hair out of your home dryer’s lint trap! You want your pet to enjoy clean, fresh bedding—let our commercial washer and dryer do the cleaning for you. Our pet laundry service is available as an add-on to your pet’s daycare reservation. Simply drop off the items you want washed when your pet checks in for daycare and pick up freshly washed toys, bedding, and blankets at the end of the day.

  • What We Can Accept for Washing

    Any items that can fit in our commercial washer and dryer are accepted for laundering at our daycare locations. These include:

    • Towels and blankets;
    • Machine-washable soft toys;
    • Small pet beds or bed covers

    If you have a specific item in mind but don’t see it listed; contact us and we’ll let you know if we can clean it for you.

  • How Pet Laundry Works

    We are only able to accept items as an add-on request for daycare reservations; this service is not available with boarding reservations at this time. Additionally:

    • Laundry items must be picked up and dropped off on the same day.
    • Please bring all items in a bag that is clearly marked with your pet’s name.
    • Laundry service may be requested through the daycare reservation software or our front desk attendant can add it at check-in.
